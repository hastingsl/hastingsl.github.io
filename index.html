About

Hi! My name is Lauren Hastings. I'm a sophomore at the University of Central Florida in Orlando studying computer science and mathematics. This semester I'm working on a literature review for computational humor generation and intend to start an independent research project this fall.

I'll be interning with Apple in Cupertino, California this summer working with back-end web technologies for the Apple TV QA team. I started my move out west last summer by interning with Intel in Chandler, Arizona. I worked in design automation with Perl, a fun departure from my previous experience in front-end web development.

Like any programmer, I don't like repeating myself, so all you'll find on this site is what you can't find on my resume and my Linkedin.

Feel free to email me or add me on Linkedin if you'd like to connect. I'd love to start doing pro-bono technical projects and consulting for non-profits, so if you know anyone who needs help or your organization would like assistance, don't hesitate to contact me!
